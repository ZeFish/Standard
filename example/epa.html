✓ Air Quality Improving

Executive Summary

Our comprehensive monitoring network tracked air quality across all 50 states, measuring key pollutants and their impact on public health.

Key Findings

  • Particulate matter (PM2.5) decreased by 15% nationally
  • Ozone levels remained stable in most regions
  • Industrial emissions showed significant reduction
  • Urban air quality improved in 87% of monitored cities

Methodology

Data was collected from 328 monitoring stations using EPA-certified equipment. All measurements follow standardized protocols established under the Clean Air Act.
